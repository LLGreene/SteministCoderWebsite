About Us

At STEMinist Coder, we believe in empowering women through technology and coding education. Our mission is to bridge the gender gap in tech by providing resources, workshops, and a supportive community for aspiring female developers.

Founded by a group of passionate engineers and developers, our platform aims to inspire the next generation of women in STEM. Join us in transforming the tech landscape, one coder at a time!
